# Move-layer deep validation — stakes-weighted (top-40 by n_threads, 2026-07-01)

**Method:** the 40 highest-count (most-witnessed) moves deep-validated on TWO axes with source access — **soundness** (is the diagnostic advice statistically correct?) + **faithfulness** (is it witnessed in the cited threads, or imported?). The 1,875 single-witness moves (95%) are the accepted searchable tail, not reviewed.

## Result — the two axes diverge cleanly
- **Soundness ≈ 39/40.** The load-bearing moves are **statistically correct as advice** — the diagnostic steps (reparameterize to fix geometry; raise adapt_delta only for divergences; pairs-plot to localize; SBC needs prior re-draws; dense metric can't fix a funnel) are canonical and right. Only ~1 minor logic note (ruling a hypothesis *out* ≠ confirming another). **→ moves are trustworthy AS ADVICE.**
- **Faithfulness is mixed (16/40 flagged)** — but with a consistent shape: the **ACTION is witnessed**, while the attached **specific `expected_findings`** (exact divergence counts, RFF timings, parameter values) are **often NOT traceable** to the readable threads — some are added synthesis, some live in unresolvable colon-form sources. Clearest case (i1): a GP move whose specific length-scale/output-scale funnel + RFF-timing findings are **absent from all 4 of its cited threads** (generic hierarchical-funnel discussions with zero GP content).

## The pattern = the project's signature, one layer down
Sound general advice + **imported specifics**. The move's **action is the durable, trustworthy content**; the specific numbers attached are **illustrative, not verified** — exactly the distiller failure mode the whole review system targets.

## Disposition
Keep the moves (actions are sound + largely witnessed); **label the specific findings as illustrative** (drawn from threads, not individually certified). Consistent with the searchable-tail design. No move-content rewrite needed; the honest framing is "witnessed diagnostic *action* + illustrative finding."
