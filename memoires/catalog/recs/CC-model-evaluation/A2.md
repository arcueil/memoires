## Technique (pymc-labs)

**Targeted test-statistic PPC (posterior-predictive p-value) + grouped/stratified PPC** — Define a targeted statistic T(y) (zero-fraction for zero-inflation, tail-fraction/max for heavy tails, skew), compute T on observed and on every posterior-predictive draw, overlay observed vs the predictive histogram (posterior-predictive p-value). Also stratified PPC (az.plot_ppc_dist(..., cols=['group'])) to catch per-subgroup misfit an aggregate PPC hides. Turns the abstract 'probe the relevant direction' into an executable check.
*Source: [pymc-labs:model-evaluation](https://github.com/pymc-labs/python-analytics-skills)*
